🚀 PitchPro - Interactive Pitch Deck BuilderPitchPro is a powerful, fully responsive, web-based presentation builder inspired by the classic Microsoft PowerPoint ribbon interface, but completely reimagined for the modern browser environment. Whether you are a founder pitching to investors, a student finalizing a report, or a business professional organizing data, PitchPro provides a robust, zero-installation platform to create, format, animate, and export professional-grade pitch decks.Built with performance and user experience in mind, PitchPro bridges the gap between complex desktop software and intuitive web applications. It offers a familiar workflow, augmented by cutting-edge AI capabilities and seamless export options, all running locally in your browser.✨ Key Features🎨 Modern Workspace & Ribbon UIClassic Ribbon Navigation: Experience a familiar, tabbed interface organized logically (Home, Insert, Design, Transitions, Animations, Slide Show, Record, Review, View) to give you immediate access to a comprehensive suite of tools without cluttering your workspace.Fully Responsive Architecture: PitchPro is designed to work wherever you do. The UI adapts flawlessly across desktop monitors, tablets, and mobile devices, utilizing smart, slide-over utility panels to maximize canvas space on smaller screens.Multiple Presentation Views: Switch effortlessly between Normal View for granular slide editing and Slide Sorter for a high-level, drag-and-drop overview of your entire deck's flow.📝 Rich Slide EditingSmart Canvas Layouts: Quickly structure your information by choosing from predefined Title, Content, and Split (Media + Text) layouts.Granular Text Formatting: Take control of your typography with standard styling options including Bold, Italic, Underline, and comprehensive paragraph alignment controls (Left, Center, Right, Justify).Seamless Media Integration: Enrich your slides visually. Upload images directly from your local drive, paste external image URLs, or embed dynamic YouTube videos straight into your presentations.Design & Theming: Establish a cohesive look instantly. Change individual slide backgrounds or apply global themes using a curated palette of premium colors.🪄 Animations & PresentationsSlide Transitions: Control the pacing of your narrative with smooth, professional transitions between slides, including Fade, Slide-Up, Zoom, Wipe, and Morph effects.Element Animations: Make your key points stand out with engaging entrance animations for specific elements (Appear, Fade, Fly-In, Zoom).Immersive Presentation Mode: Deliver your pitch flawlessly with a built-in, full-screen presentation mode that removes all distractions and supports standard keyboard navigation (Arrow keys, Spacebar, Escape).🤖 AI-Powered Tools (Powered by Gemini)Intelligent Spelling & Grammar Check: Never miss a typo again. PitchPro leverages the Gemini AI model to automatically review your slide copy, identifying and fixing grammatical errors while preserving your original tone and meaning.Universal Translator: Break down language barriers instantly. With a single click, translate the entire text content of your current slide into over 100 global languages, making your pitch accessible to international audiences.💾 High-Fidelity Export OptionsExport to Native PPTX: Need to share your file or continue editing offline? PitchPro compiles your web-based slides into a fully editable, native Microsoft PowerPoint (.pptx) file.Export to High-Quality PDF: Generate a perfectly scaled, 16:9 (1920x1080) high-resolution PDF document of your presentation, ideal for emailing, printing, or formal archiving.🛠️ Tech StackPitchPro is built using a modern, fast, and scalable technology stack:Core Framework: React 18 for building a dynamic, component-based user interface.Styling & UI: Tailwind CSS for rapid, utility-first styling and ensuring deep responsiveness.Iconography: Lucide React for crisp, consistent, and customizable vector icons.PDF Generation Engine: html2pdf.js utilized for rendering the DOM into high-resolution, paginated PDF documents.PowerPoint Compilation: PptxGenJS integrated to programmatically build native .pptx files directly in the browser.AI Integration: Google Gemini API powering the advanced grammar correction and real-time translation features.🚀 Getting StartedFollow these instructions to get a copy of the project up and running on your local machine for development and testing purposes.PrerequisitesEnsure you have the following installed on your system:Node.js (v16 or higher recommended)npm or yarn package managerInstallationClone the repository:git clone [https://github.com/yourusername/pitchpro.git](https://github.com/yourusername/pitchpro.git)
cd pitchpro
Install dependencies:npm install
Configure Tailwind CSS:Ensure your tailwind.config.js is set up properly to scan your React files. The project relies on Tailwind for all styling. Verify that your base CSS file (e.g., src/App.css or src/index.css) imports the Tailwind directives:@tailwind base;
@tailwind components;
@tailwind utilities;
Add your Gemini API Key:To enable the AI Grammar and Translation features, you must provide a valid Gemini API key.Open src/App.jsx.Locate the performGeminiTask function.Add your API key to the apiKey variable:const apiKey = "YOUR_GEMINI_API_KEY_HERE";
Start the development server:npm run dev
Open your browser:Navigate to http://localhost:5173 (or the port provided by your Vite terminal output) to view the application.💡 Usage GuideAdding Slides: Click the + New Slide button located in the Home tab of the ribbon or on the left sidebar thumbnail view.Changing Layouts: Navigate to the Home tab and use the layout selectors, or utilize the floating quick-access toolbar directly above the main canvas to switch between Title, Content, and Split layouts.Inserting Media: Go to the Insert tab on the ribbon.
